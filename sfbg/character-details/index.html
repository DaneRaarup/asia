Character Details for So Far from the Bamboo Grove

March 14th, 2017

This page contains information about characters from the book So Far from the Bamboo Grove.

The characters that will be included in this are Yoko (the main character), her mother, Ko (her sister), Hideyo, and Corporal Matsumura (a friend of the family).

Photos are being added and will be here soon.

Yoko

March 15th, 2017

Strength: Yoko's strength is her ability to learn skills that she would never have foreseen herself using prior to leaving the bamboo grove.

Weakness: Her weakness lies in her introvertedness and how scared she is, especially at the beginning of the book.

Defining Moment: Her defining moment is when she wins the writing contest to earn money for her sister and herself.

Essential Question: Why is she not more scared to go to the school with the wealthy Japanese girls?

Image Meaning: The image of a rag doll represents the view of the other girls of the Japanese school on Yoko.

Image is in public domain.

Mother

March 15th, 2017

Strength: Yoko's mother's strength is her constant desire to protect her children.

Weakness: Her mother's weakness is that she has difficulty finding a reason to stay alive when her parents died in the bombing.

Defining Moment: Yoko and Ko's mother's defining moment is when she safely brings her children into Japan.

Essential Question: Why did she lose the will to live when her parents were dead, even though her children needed her?

Image Meaning: The police officers represent Mother's protective nature over her children.

Image is in public domain.

Ko

March 15th, 2017

Strength: Ko's strength is that she can protect Yoko even when their mother dies.

Weakness: Her weakness lies in the fact that she does not understand Yoko's importance.

Defining Moment: Ko's defining moment is when her mother dies and she must be the head of the family.

Essential Question: Why does she wish Yoko dead and treat Yoko so badly when they needed each other to survive in Japan?

Image Meaning: The boss that works in this office space represents how Ko is bossy with Yoko.

Image is in public domain.

Hideyo

March 15th, 2017

Strength: His strength is that he wants very badly to see his family again.

Weakness: Hideyo's weakness is that he seemed to want to join the student army, missing his family's departure.

Defining Moment: His defining moment is when he makes it to the farmhouse and he is accepted as a brother there.

Essential Question: Why did he fall to the social pressure to join the student army (and then get all of the questions wrong), when it almost cost him ever seeing his family again?

Image Meaning: The friends in the image represent Hideyo's relationship with the Korean family that accepted him.

Image is by Faisal Akram and is licensed under CC BY SA 2.0 (via Wikimedia Commons).

Corporal Matsumura

March 15th, 2017

Strength: His strength is that Corporal Matsumura felt the need to see Yoko's family again, and went to great lengths to do so.

Weakness: The corporal's weakness is that he did not feel the will to live until Yoko saw him for the first time.

Defining Moment: His defining moment is when he escapes on the log for four days to see the family.

Essential Question: Why did he suddenly gain the will to live when Yoko promised to dance for him?

Image Meaning: The hard working, motivated soldiers in this image represent the corporal's motivation to see Yoko and her family.

Image is in public domain in Japan, however copyright was restored in the United States under the URAA. It was painted in Japan by Toshi Shimizu in 1944 (the time period of the book, too).
